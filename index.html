About Sea

The sea is a vast body of salt water that covers most of the Earth’s surface and plays an important role in human life and nature.
It provides food, supports transportation and trade, and helps regulate the Earth’s climate.
Seas are home to a wide variety of marine life, including fish, whales, corals, and tiny organisms that maintain the balance of the ecosystem.
People use the sea for fishing, tourism, and other economic activities, but pollution, overfishing, and climate change are causing serious harm to marine environments.
Protecting the sea through conservation and responsible use is essential for the future of our planet and all living beings.
